### **DCL (Data Control Language)**
  * **GRANT:** Giving users permissions.
  * **REVOKE:** Removing users' permissions.

### **TCL (Transaction Control Language)**
  * **COMMIT:** Permanently saving changes.
  * **ROLLBACK:** Undoing changes since the last COMMIT or start of a transaction.
  * **SAVEPOINT:** Setting a point within a transaction to rollback to.
